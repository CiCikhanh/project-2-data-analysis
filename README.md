# Data Analysis: Sleep vs Energy 📊

## Goal
Analyze how sleep affects energy levels.

## Tools
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

## Result
There is a positive correlation between sleep and energy.

## Insight
Getting 7–9 hours of sleep improves daily performance.
This analysis shows a strong positive correlation between sleep and energy.

From personal observation:
- Days with less than 6 hours of sleep → low energy
- Days with 7–8 hours → significantly better performance

## Conclusion
Sleep directly impacts daily productivity and mental state.
